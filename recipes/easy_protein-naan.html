Easy Protein Naan

This is a quick and easy Naan recipe that requires no yeast or proofing - just mix it all up and cook!

Time

Takes about 10mins to make the dough, and another 10 to cook it up. 20mins total.

Serving Size

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup Protein Yogurt
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Add 1 cup protein yogurt and 1 cup water to a large bowl. Mix well.
  2. Add the 4 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, and 2 tsp baking soda.
  3. Mix together until just combined. The dough will be sticky and flakey, that is normal.
  4. Fold the dough over a few times before turning onto a floured surface.
  5. Form the dough into a ball shape by dragging it accross the surface of the four. Add more flour if necessary.
  6. Roll out the dough into a longer shape, then cut into chunks, around 12-18 depending on the size.
  7. Roll out the mini-chunks of dough into flat disks on a floured surface.
  8. Meanwhile, heat up a large flat pan. Once hot, add some olive oil and butter.
  9. Cook the naan disks on the pan, around 5 minutes on each side, or until air bubbles form.
  10. Serve as is, or brush with additional butter before serving.

    11. Notes

    • You can make this Naan into garlic Naan by sprinkling finely chopped garlic onto the naan right before cooking.
    • You can add cilantro to the Naan before serving for a fresh flavor.
    • Will keep in fridge about 3 days. Warm up in oven before serving.
